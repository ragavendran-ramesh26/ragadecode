How We Work

We use a unique hybrid model of human editorial judgment supported by AI-assisted research tools.

Here’s what our content creation workflow looks like:

  1. Discover: We track trending topics across YouTube, news media, and social feeds.
  2. Transcribe: We convert long-form videos (especially Tamil commentary) into readable text.
  3. Verify: Using tools AI tools, we verify claims, filter misinformation, and identify bias.
  4. Decode: We rewrite the story in a structured, referenceable article — with proper sourcing and clarity.
  5. Review: Final publishing is manual, reviewed by a real editor — never AI-only.
