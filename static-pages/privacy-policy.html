Effective Date: July 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how RagaDecode.com ("we", "our", or "the site") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit or interact with our website. By using the site, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

We use Google Analytics and similar tools to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as:

  • Pages visited

This data helps us understand user behavior and improve our content. Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you.

3. Cookies Policy

We may use cookies (small text files stored on your device) to:

Note: Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

You can manage your ad personalization settings or opt out by visiting:
