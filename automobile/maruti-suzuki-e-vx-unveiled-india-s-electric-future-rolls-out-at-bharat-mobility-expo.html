Maruti Suzuki eVX Unveiled: India’s Electric Future Rolls Out at Bharat Mobility Expo

Published on: 22 Jun 2025

The Bharat Mobility Global Expo became the stage for a bold step into India's electric future as Maruti Suzuki showcased the eVX electric SUV. This futuristic concept vehicle hints at the automaker’s commitment to sustainable mobility and innovation tailored to Indian roads.

Maruti Suzuki eVX: A Glimpse Into Tomorrow

Maruti Suzuki’s eVX electric SUV, displayed at the event, instantly captured attention with its cutting-edge design and advanced EV features. Positioned to compete in the mid-size SUV segment, the eVX combines performance, range, and tech that resonate with the evolving preferences of urban Indian drivers.

Key Specifications

  • Powertrain Options:

    • 49 kW and 61 kW electric motor variants
    • Torque output: 192.5 Nm

  • Range:

    • Up to 300 km – 500 km, depending on the variant

  • Charging Capabilities:

    • DC Fast Charging
    • Standard AC Charging Support

  • Size and Build:

    • Length: Approx. 4.3 meters
    • Likely equipped with 225/55 R17 alloy wheels

Standout Features & Tech

Maruti has left no stone unturned to bring premium features to this EV SUV:

  • Panoramic Sunroof – Letting in more light and luxury
  • 360-Degree Camera – For complete visibility and safety
  • Touchscreen Infotainment System – With an Infinity sound system
  • Ambient Lighting – Setting the mood for every drive
  • ORVM Tech – Enhanced side mirror tech with integrated features
  • Digital Display Cluster – Possibly extended with HUD-like transparency for concept variant

Built for Indian Roads, Backed by Global Tech

The eVX is designed not only to be futuristic but also practical. From its elegant front LED DRLs to Maruti Suzuki's new EV emblem, everything signals a shift in the company’s design philosophy. With SUV proportions and EV dynamics, it is aimed at the urban, aspirational buyer segment.

No Official Launch Date Yet

While Maruti Suzuki has not confirmed a release timeline, industry insiders suggest production models may arrive as early as 2025, making this one of the most anticipated electric SUVs in India.

Final Thoughts

Maruti Suzuki’s eVX is not just a concept—it’s a vision. A vision that aligns with India’s electric mobility roadmap, promising a cleaner, smarter, and more efficient driving experience.

With its futuristic design, tech-first features, and brand reliability, the eVX is poised to be a game changer in India's electric vehicle revolution.
