Fatal Accident at Chennai Ambattur Junction: Residents Demand Urgent Safety Measures

Published on: 6 Jul 2025

A tragic road accident occurred on July 5, 2025, in Vijayalakshmipuram, Ambattur, Chennai. A speeding car reportedly hit a woman riding a two-wheeler, leading to her death on the spot. This has triggered outrage and fresh demands from local residents for immediate installation of speed breakers at the accident-prone four-road junction.

What Happened

According to a handwritten petition submitted by area residents, the accident took place around 6:00 PM at the four-way intersection of Anna Salai, Ambedkar Street, Cheran Street, and other adjoining roads in Vijayalakshmipuram. A white electric car, bearing the license plate TN13AJ5022, is alleged to have collided with a woman on a two-wheeler (TN13H4741), causing fatal injuries.

The woman, as stated in the letter, succumbed to her injuries immediately at the spot. Photos from the scene show visible damage to the vehicles involved and the surroundings.

Community‚Äôs Outcry

Following the incident, over 28 local residents signed a petition addressed to the Chief Engineer of the Chennai Corporation, Road Department (Zone 7). In the letter, they highlight:

  • This is not the first accident at this junction.
  • Multiple previous complaints about speeding and traffic risks were ignored.
  • Repeated appeals for speed breakers were never acted upon.
  • Residents now live in fear due to frequent accidents in the area.

The petition emphasizes the urgent need for speed breakers on all four sides of the junction to prevent further tragedies.
