The resignation of Additional Superintendent of Police N. V. Baramani in Karnataka has ignited a fierce debate on the dignity of public servants. His decision to step down followed an alleged public humiliation by Chief Minister Siddaramaiah during a political rally. The Incident: A Moment of Public Humiliation
