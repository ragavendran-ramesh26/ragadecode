CBI Intensifies Probe in Ajith Kumar Custodial Death Case: Timeline, Charges, and Latest Developments

Published on: 16 Jul 2025

In a chilling reminder of systemic custodial violence in India, the tragic death of 27-year-old temple guard Ajith Kumar while in police custody has sparked widespread outrage. The case, rooted in the Sivaganga district of Tamil Nadu, has now reached national significance as the Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) officially leads the probe under murder charges. This article decodes the entire timeline, charges, and current status of the case, along with fresh evidence, legal directives, and what lies ahead.

Who Was Ajith Kumar?

Ajith Kumar served as a night security guard at the Madapuram Bathrakaliamman Temple in Thiruppuvanam. Known for his humble background and clean record, Ajith's name surfaced in a verbal complaint regarding stolen temple jewellery. Ajith was picked up by the local police without a formal FIR and brought to the Thiruppuvanam police station. He was reportedly interrogated overnight, subjected to chilli powder torture, ganja smoke inhalation, and physical assault. His mother, who met him that night, described him as shivering and pleading innocence.

June 28, 2025

Ajith collapsed during transit to the hospital and was declared dead. Initial reports cited medical distress, but a preliminary autopsy later revealed 44 external injuries, brain hemorrhage, and signs of cigarette burns.
