The incident took place at the De Schorre recreation area in Boom, Belgium, the traditional venue for Tomorrowland festival. Known for its picturesque landscapes and massive infrastructure, this venue has hosted the festival since 2005.

Authorities have ruled out malicious intent or sabotage as of now, but the Tomorrowland 2025 fire has raised questions about:
