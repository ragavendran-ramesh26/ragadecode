Quick Info

Who was Madhan Bob?
Madhan Bob, born S. Krishnamoorthy, was a veteran Tamil actor and comedian renowned for his expressive acting and comic timing. He appeared in nearly 700 films and was also a respected television personality and musician.
When did Madhan Bob pass away?
Madhan Bob passed away on August 2, 2025, at the age of 71. He died of cancer at his residence in Adyar, Chennai around 5:00 PM IST.
What was Madhan Bob's real name?
His birth name was S. Krishnamoorthy. He later adopted the stage name Madhan Bob, which was coined during a comic duo phase with his brother.
How did Madhan Bob get his stage name?
The name 'Bob' was given by filmmaker K. Balachander when Madhan Bob performed as part of a comic duo with his brother, leading to the duo being known as 'Madhan and Bob.'
What were Madhan Bob's early career beginnings?
He began his career as a musician, trained in Western classical and Carnatic music. He composed music for stage plays and television jingles, mentoring A.R. Rahman early on.
Which musical legend did Madhan Bob mentor?
Madhan Bob mentored A.R. Rahman, who worked as his keyboard assistant early in his career.
What was Madhan Bob best known for in film?
He was best known for his comic roles in Tamil cinema, with notable performances in films like 'Thenali', 'Friends', 'Thevar Magan', and 'Vasool Raja MBBS'.
What television show was Madhan Bob famous for?
He was widely recognized for his role as a judge on the comedy show 'Asatha Povathu Yaaru?' aired on Sun TV.
How many films did Madhan Bob appear in?
He appeared in approximately 700 films across Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, and Malayalam languages, with over 650 in Tamil alone.
