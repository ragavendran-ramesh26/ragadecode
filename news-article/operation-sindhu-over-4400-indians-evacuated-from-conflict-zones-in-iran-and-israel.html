Operation SINDHU: Over 4,400 Indians Evacuated from Conflict Zones in Iran and Israel

Published on: 27 Jun 2025

New Delhi In a significant humanitarian effort, the Government of India has successfully evacuated 4,415 individuals from conflict-hit regions of Iran and Israel as part of Operation SINDHU. A special evacuation flight from Yerevan, Armenia, landed in Delhi today, carrying 173 Indian nationals to safety.

Latest Flight Brings Relief

The most recent group of evacuees included Indian citizens fleeing instability in Iran, where tensions have escalated in recent weeks. The aircraft, arranged as part of India’s emergency response efforts, marked another critical milestone in the ongoing mission.

Breakdown of Evacuations

Here’s a breakdown of the individuals rescued under Operation SINDHU:

  • 3,597 evacuated from Iran
  • 818 evacuated from Israel
  • Additional evacuations include:
    • 14 OCI (Overseas Citizen of India) cardholders
    • 9 Nepali nationals
    • 4 Sri Lankan nationals
    • 1 Iranian spouse of an Indian national

This reflects India’s commitment not only to its citizens but also to international humanitarian coordination.

Voices from the Ground

Several evacuees shared emotional accounts of their ordeal and praised the swift government response:

“We had planned to shift to another city because the situation was worsening. Thankfully, the embassy helped us immediately.”

“Our parents were very anxious. We’re grateful to be back on Indian soil.”

Many highlighted the support provided by Indian missions abroad, including shelter, communication, and logistical assistance.

India's Global Rescue Network

Operation SINDHU is the latest in a series of successful evacuation missions conducted by India, following Vande Bharat, Operation Ganga, and Operation Devi Shakti. It showcases India’s growing diplomatic capability and readiness to act swiftly in global crises.

The Ministry of External Affairs, in coordination with Indian embassies in Tehran, Tel Aviv, and Yerevan, continues to monitor the situation and stands prepared for further assistance if needed.

Conclusion

Operation SINDHU reaffirms India’s unwavering commitment to the safety of its citizens across the globe. As regional tensions persist, India's swift response and humanitarian focus remain a beacon of national resolve.
