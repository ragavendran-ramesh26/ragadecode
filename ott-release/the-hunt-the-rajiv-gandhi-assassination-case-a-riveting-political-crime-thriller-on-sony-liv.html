The Hunt: The Rajiv Gandhi Assassination Case – A Riveting Political Crime Thriller on SonyLIV

Published on: 11 Jul 2025

“The Hunt: The Rajiv Gandhi Assassination Case” is a gripping political-crime drama that revisits the 1991 assassination of India’s former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi and the intense manhunt that followed. Based on the true events described in the book “Ninety Days” by Anirudhya Mitra (a former CBI officer), the series offers an immersive retelling of one of India's most shocking political murders.

Storyline

The show begins with the tragic suicide bombing at Sriperumbudur on May 21, 1991, carried out by an LTTE operative, which resulted in the death of Rajiv Gandhi. The narrative follows the Special Investigation Team (SIT) led by D.R. Kaarthikeyan, assigned to crack the conspiracy under extreme pressure and tight deadlines. The investigation uncovers:

  • Intelligence failures
  • Political interference
  • International cross-border movements
  • Ground-level pursuit of suspects
  • The emotional and professional toll on investigators

The series documents 90 intense days of forensic analysis, coordinated raids, and relentless pursuit, making it an unmissable watch for fans of historical crime and procedural thrillers.
