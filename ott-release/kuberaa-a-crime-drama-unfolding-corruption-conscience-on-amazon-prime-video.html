Kuberaa – A Crime Drama Unfolding Corruption & Conscience on Amazon Prime Video

Published on: 17 Jul 2025

Kuberaa, directed by Sekhar Kammula, is a gripping crime drama that unfolds against the backdrop of India's corrupt financial and political systems. With powerhouse performances by Dhanush, Nagarjuna, and Rashmika Mandanna, the film combines raw intensity with emotional depth, making it one of 2025’s most talked-about OTT releases.

Storyline

The story follows Deva Kallam (Dhanush), a beggar from Tirupati who becomes entangled in a massive money-laundering conspiracy involving oil reserves and high-ranking officials. Led by former CBI officer Deepak Tej (Nagarjuna) and businessman Neeraj Mitra, the plan uses beggars as a front for illicit transactions. But when Deva decides to break free, the hunter becomes the hunted in this dark game of survival and justice.

Reviews & Reception

Critical Acclaim

  • The Times of India: “Kuberaa is a gripping crime drama with a philosophical edge.”
  • The News Minute: “Dhanush and Nagarjuna shine in this layered narrative that explores power and exploitation.”
  • GreatAndhra: “Bold story, powerful performances, and a compelling narrative arc.”

Audience Feedback

  • Viewers praised Dhanush’s performance as “career-best.”
  • Some found the second half slower, but the emotional climax was appreciated.
  • Rating on Rotten Tomatoes: 78% (Critics), 8.3/10 (Audience)

Box Office & Streaming Success

Released theatrically on June 20, 2025, the film grossed over ₹132 crore globally. Its digital release on Amazon Prime Video on July 18, 2025 saw a surge in viewership, especially in South India and among political thriller fans.

Why Watch Kuberaa?

  • Dhanush delivers a career-defining performance
  • Tackles relevant social and economic issues
  • Stunning visuals with 4K Ultra HD and Dolby Atmos sound
  • A rare blend of gritty storytelling and emotional depth

Final Verdict

Kuberaa is not just a crime drama—it’s a reflection of today’s broken systems and the individuals who dare to challenge them. With high production value, strong writing, and committed performances, this film deserves your watchlist spot in 2025.
