Maargan – Release Date, Key Details & Expectations

Published on: 3 Aug 2025

The theatrical film Maargan debuted on 27 June 2025, delivering a suspense-filled supernatural crime thriller that earned positive early reactions. Directed, written, and edited by Leo John Paul in his directorial debut, this genre-blending movie explores deep-rooted trauma and prejudice in a chilling investigative setup.

Storyline: What to Expect

Set after a personal tragedy, ADGP Dhruv (Vijay Antony) arrives in Chennai to investigate a series of brutal murders where victims are injected with a serum that blackens them before death. As he delves into the case, he discovers eerie connections to his own past and ancient Siddhar lore.

  • Arc 1: Dhruv tracks Tamilarivu, a swimmer with eidetic memory and ties to temple rituals.
  • Arc 2: Flashback-driven revelations expose corruption, trauma, and prejudice surrounding skin color.
  • Arc 3: Climax reveals Vennila / Mega as the killer, with a tragic psychological motive tied to colorism and identity.

Final Verdict

Maargan modestly surpasses expectations as a taut, smart supernatural crime thriller, blending procedural rigor with emotional and social layers. It succeeds as a thoughtful genre hybrid, with Vijay Antony’s multiple creative contributions anchoring the film. OTT audiences will experience suspenseful investigative drama with a haunting commentary on trauma and identity.

Quick Info

What is the film Maargan about?
Maargan is a Tamil-language supernatural crime thriller centered on ADGP Dhruv, who investigates mysterious murders in Chennai that involve victims being injected with a blackening serum. As he delves deeper, he uncovers a personal connection to the crimes and an ancient Siddhar legend, blending suspense, social commentary, and trauma.
Who directed Maargan?
Maargan was directed by Leo John Paul, who also served as the film’s writer and editor. This marks his directorial debut.
When was Maargan released?
Maargan was theatrically released on 27 June 2025. It later premiered on OTT platforms starting 25 July 2025.
Where can I watch Maargan online?
Maargan is available for streaming on Amazon Prime Video in India and Tentkotta for international audiences, starting from 25 July 2025.
What genre does Maargan fall under?
Maargan is a genre-blending film that combines crime thriller and supernatural elements with social commentary, particularly on trauma and colorism.
What is the runtime of Maargan?
The film has a runtime of 132 minutes, which is approximately 2 hours and 12 minutes.
Who plays the lead role in Maargan?
Vijay Antony plays the lead role of ADGP Dhruv Guvarak in Maargan.
What is the age rating for Maargan?
Maargan is rated UA (13+), which is roughly equivalent to a PG-13 rating.
What was Maargan's budget and box office collection?
Maargan was made on a budget of ₹12 crore and collected ₹14 crore, making it a minor box office hit.
Who composed the music for Maargan?
The music for Maargan was composed by Vijay Antony, who also played the lead role.
Who is the killer in Maargan?
The killer is revealed to be Vennila, also known as Mega. Her motive is tied to deep psychological trauma and issues of identity and colorism.
Does Maargan involve supernatural themes?
Yes, the film includes supernatural elements, particularly tied to Siddhar lore and mysterious rituals surrounding the murders.
What languages is Maargan available in?
Maargan was originally made in Tamil and has dubbed versions in Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Hindi.

The serum used in the murders blackens the victims before death, symbolizing deeper themes of prejudice and connecting to ancient lore, which is central to the film’s mystery.
