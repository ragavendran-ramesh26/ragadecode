DNA (Tamil) – A Genetic Thriller with a Punch OTT Release Jio Hotstar

Published on: 17 Jul 2025

DNA, directed by Nelson Venkatesan, is a gritty Tamil action-thriller that explores the dangerous interplay between crime, genetics, and family secrets. With Atharvaa Murali in the lead, the film promises high-octane sequences, emotional depth, and a gripping narrative that delves into identity and legacy.

Storyline

Set against a dark urban backdrop, DNA follows Arjun (Atharvaa Murali), a forensic genetics expert who becomes entangled in a series of mysterious murders connected by a cryptic DNA strand. As Arjun delves deeper, he uncovers secrets buried within his own family lineage, forcing him to confront past traumas while racing against time to stop a killer using science as a weapon.

Cast Highlights

  • Atharvaa Murali as Arjun
  • Sarath Kumar as DCP Ravichandran
  • Smruthi Venkat as Divya
  • Jayaprakash, Mime Gopi, and Suresh Chakravarthy in key roles
  • Director: Nelson Venkatesan
  • Music: Sam C.S.
  • Cinematography: Dinesh Krishnan
  • Editing: R. Nirmal

Reviews & Reception

Critical Acclaim

  • Behindwoods: “A smart, intense thriller that doesn't dumb down its science.”
  • Cinema Vikatan: “Atharvaa delivers a career-best in this DNA-driven thriller.”
  • Film Companion South: “Emotion meets intellect in Nelson’s tight narrative.”

Audience Feedback

  • Strong praise for Atharvaa’s matured performance
  • Appreciation for the realistic science elements in the plot
  • Some viewers felt the second act dragged, but climax was highly praised
  • IMDb Rating: 8.0/10 (early reviews)

Box Office & Streaming Success

Released theatrically on July 5, 2025, DNA had a modest box office run but gained momentum through word-of-mouth. The OTT premiere on JioCinema/Disney+ JioHotstar on July 19, 2025, led to a surge in views, especially among thriller fans and tech-savvy audiences.

Why Watch DNA?

  • Smartly written thriller based on real scientific concepts
  • Atharvaa Murali’s most intense role yet
  • A rare Tamil film combining crime, genetics, and emotional family drama
  • Strong visuals and gripping score by Sam C.S.

Final Verdict

DNA is a sharp, high-concept Tamil thriller that doesn’t just thrill—it makes you think. Nelson Venkatesan weaves action and emotion with scientific intrigue, making it one of the must-watch South Indian thrillers of 2025.
