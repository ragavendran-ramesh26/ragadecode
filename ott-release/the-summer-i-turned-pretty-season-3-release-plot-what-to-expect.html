The Summer I Turned Pretty Season 3 – Release, Plot & What to Expect

Published on: 17 Jul 2025

Jenny Han’s beloved coming-of-age series returns for a third season, promising another wave of emotional highs, love triangles, and seaside nostalgia. After a gripping Season 2 finale, The Summer I Turned Pretty – Season 3 is set to deepen the story of Belly, Conrad, and Jeremiah as they navigate life, love, and growing up.

Storyline

Season 3 will pick up from the emotional cliffhanger of Season 2, where Belly made a life-changing choice between the Fisher brothers. With college on the horizon, the stakes are higher than ever — not just for romance, but for identity, friendship, and family.

Expect the new season to draw heavily from Jenny Han’s third novel, We’ll Always Have Summer, exploring themes of maturity, heartbreak, and second chances.

Cast Highlights

Returning Cast:

  • Lola Tung as Belly Conklin
  • Christopher Briney as Conrad Fisher
  • Gavin Casalegno as Jeremiah Fisher
  • Sean Kaufman as Steven Conklin
  • Rain Spencer as Taylor
  • Jackie Chung as Laurel Conklin
  • David Iacono as Cam

Possible New Additions:

  • New characters from Belly’s college life may be introduced.

Filming Locations

Season 3 is expected to be filmed again in Wilmington, North Carolina, standing in for the fictional Cousins Beach. New locations, including college campuses, may also be introduced.

Public Reaction and Hype

The series has built a strong fan base among Gen Z and young adults. The teaser confirmation of Season 3 sent fans into a frenzy, with many hoping for closure to Belly’s complex love story.

Notable Fan Reactions:

  • "I need to know who Belly ends up with NOW!"
  • "Jenny Han always knows how to crush and heal my heart."
  • "Season 3 better do justice to Jeremiah."

Previous Seasons Overview

Season 1

  • Released: June 2022
  • Plot: Belly’s annual summer at Cousins Beach reignites childhood friendships and love triangles.
  • Known for: Wholesome storytelling, nostalgic aesthetic, and a killer indie-pop soundtrack.

Season 2

  • Released: July 2023
  • Plot: After a tragic loss, relationships are tested and choices become permanent.
  • Known for: Emotional maturity, deeper conflicts, and character development.

Final Verdict

The Summer I Turned Pretty Season 3 is poised to be the most emotionally resonant season yet. With more episodes, deeper themes, and higher stakes, it’s not just another summer — it’s a turning point. Whether you're Team Conrad or Team Jeremiah, get ready for a heartfelt ride.
