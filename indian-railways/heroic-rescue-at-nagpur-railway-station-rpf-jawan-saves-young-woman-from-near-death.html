Heroic Rescue at Nagpur Railway Station: RPF Jawan Saves Young Woman from Near-Death

Published on: 4 Jul 2025

In a heart-stopping incident at Maharashtra’s Nagpur railway station, a young woman’s life was saved by the quick action of a Railway Protection Force (RPF) jawan. As part of Operation Jeevan Raksha, this incident showcases the critical role of the RPF in ensuring passenger safety on India’s vast railway network.

The Incident

While trying to board a moving train at Nagpur station, the woman slipped and fell dangerously close to the tracks. The mishap could have ended in tragedy, but fate had other plans thanks to one vigilant officer.

The Brave Act

An RPF jawan, stationed nearby, saw the woman fall and sprang into action. Without losing a moment, he pulled her away from the train, just in time to save her life. His courage, presence of mind, and quick reflexes were nothing short of heroic.

Operation Jeevan Raksha

This rescue effort is part of Operation Jeevan Raksha, a national initiative led by the Railway Protection Force to prevent accidents and protect lives across Indian Railways. Time and again, RPF officers have shown outstanding bravery in situations that demand immediate action.

Conclusion

Thanks to the RPF jawan’s heroic effort, a young life was saved at Nagpur station. These real-life acts of bravery serve as powerful reminders of the RPF’s dedication and the need for passenger caution while boarding or deboarding moving trains.
