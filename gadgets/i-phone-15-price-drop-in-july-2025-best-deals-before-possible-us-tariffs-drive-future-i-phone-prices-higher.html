iPhone 15 in July 2025: What’s New and What You Should Know

Should You Buy iPhone 15 Now or Wait?

Buy Now If:

  • You’re on iPhone 12 or older
  • You need reliable performance with Apple’s ecosystem
  • You want flagship-level camera and build without the Pro price
  • You value great exchange and EMI options

Wait If:

  • You want a 120Hz display or telephoto zoom
  • You're waiting for iPhone 16 or a design overhaul
  • You want USB-C with faster data and charging speed

Global Alert: How US Import Tariffs May Push iPhone Prices Higher

One of the most critical reasons to consider buying iPhone 15 now is the looming risk of a 25% U.S. import tariff on China-assembled iPhones.

What’s Happening?

  • The U.S. government is reviewing a proposal to implement tariffs on tech products not made in America, with a direct focus on Apple.
  • If the tariffs take effect by August 2025, Apple may be forced to adjust global pricing, including its pricing in India.
  • Even if India’s production remains unaffected, imported components and logistics costs could rise, affecting iPhone 16 and newer models.

Why This Matters Now

  • The iPhone 15 is largely produced in both China and India, helping shield it from price hikes in the short term.
  • Upcoming models may not enjoy the same pricing advantage, especially if new tariffs disrupt Apple's cost strategy.
  • Experts predict Apple could shift the pricing structure upward to protect margins on future models.

Bottom Line:

If the proposed U.S. tariffs become policy, iPhone 16 could launch at ₹80,000 or more, and older iPhones may see a pullback in discounts.

Final Verdict: Best Time to Buy iPhone 15 Is Now

The iPhone 15 delivers the best combination of flagship features, premium build, and long-term value in its price segment today.

With Amazon Prime Day bringing record-low prices, extra bank discounts, and increasing concerns over global pricing volatility, this is likely the best time to buy an iPhone 15 before the market shifts again.
