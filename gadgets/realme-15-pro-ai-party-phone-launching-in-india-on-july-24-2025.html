Realme 15 Pro – “AI Party Phone” Launching in India on July 24, 2025

Published on: 16 Jul 2025

The Realme 15 Pro is positioning itself as one of the most intelligent and feature-packed AI phones in India. Designed for the next generation of mobile users, this smartphone combines advanced artificial intelligence tools, voice-powered editing, dynamic camera tuning, and a premium design—all at a mid-range price. Whether you're a content creator, a mobile gamer, or someone who wants a smarter camera and smoother performance, the Realme 15 Pro is being marketed as the "AI Party Phone"—and for good reason.

It introduces innovations like AI Edit Genie for hands-free photo editing, AI Party Mode for automatic camera optimization, and a Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 processor capable of running AI tasks efficiently on-device. With its massive 7,000 mAh battery, curved AMOLED display, and flagship-grade optics, Realme is clearly setting a new benchmark in the Indian smartphone market for AI phones under ₹35,000.

Top Questions Answered

1. Is Realme 15 Pro really an AI phone?
Yes. It features on-device AI capabilities like voice-powered photo editing and scene recognition for intelligent camera adjustment.

2. How does AI Edit Genie work?
Say commands such as "brighten photo" or "remove object" and the phone automatically applies edits using its built-in AI.

3. Is the phone bulky because of the 7,000 mAh battery?
No. The device is only 7.69 mm thin—among the slimmest in its category with this battery capacity.

4. Can I expand storage with a memory card?
No. It lacks microSD support, so choose the 256 GB model if you need more space.

5. Is EMI available without a credit card?
Yes. You can apply for EMI using Bajaj Finserv, HDFC EasyEMI, TVS Credit, or Realme FlexPay without needing a credit card.

Who Should Buy This?

  • Content Creators: Use AI Edit Genie to edit social content hands-free.
  • Students and Social Users: Capture better low-light group photos with AI Party Mode.
  • Mobile Gamers: Enjoy smooth 120 FPS gaming with AI Game Coach 2.0.
  • Battery-Heavy Users: Get up to 2 days of usage on a single charge.

Final Verdict

The Realme 15 Pro sets a new standard for AI phones in India under ₹35,000. It combines smart features like voice-editing and real-time camera optimization with flagship-grade performance, display, and battery. If you're looking for a future-ready smartphone that brings AI power to your everyday life—without the flagship price—this is the AI phone to watch in 2025.

Quick Info

What is the Realme 15 Pro?
The Realme 15 Pro is a mid-range AI-powered smartphone launched in India, designed for content creators, mobile gamers, and users seeking smart camera and performance features. It includes voice-command editing, AI-enhanced photography, and a large battery.
When is the Realme 15 Pro launching in India?
The official India launch is scheduled for July 24, 2025, with pre-orders starting on July 18, 2025, exclusively on Flipkart.
What is the price of the Realme 15 Pro in India?
The expected price is ₹27,999–₹29,999 for the 128 GB model and ₹31,999–₹33,999 for the 256 GB model.
What processor does the Realme 15 Pro use?
It is powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 processor, which supports efficient on-device AI tasks.
How big is the battery on the Realme 15 Pro?
It features a massive 7,000 mAh battery, offering up to two days of usage on a single charge.
What display features does the Realme 15 Pro offer?
The phone comes with a 6.7″ FHD+ 4D Curved AMOLED display, 144 Hz refresh rate, 2,500 Hz touch sampling rate, and 6,500 nits peak brightness.
What are the camera specifications of the Realme 15 Pro?
The rear camera setup includes a 50 MP Sony IMX890 sensor with OIS, an ultra-wide lens, and a telephoto lens. The front camera is a 32 MP punch-hole selfie shooter.
Does the Realme 15 Pro support 5G?
The excerpt does not specify 5G support, but given the Snapdragon 7 Gen 4 chipset, 5G capability is likely.
What AI features does the Realme 15 Pro have?
It includes AI Edit Genie for voice-controlled photo editing, AI Party Mode for scene optimization, and AI Game Coach 2.0 for performance and thermal tuning.
What is AI Edit Genie on the Realme 15 Pro?
AI Edit Genie lets users perform hands-free edits like 'remove background' or 'add neon effect' using voice commands.
What is AI Party Mode in the Realme 15 Pro?
AI Party Mode provides real-time scene detection for automatically optimizing photo and video capture during events or group shots.
What is AI Game Coach 2.0?
It is an AI-based system that tunes game performance and manages thermal output for smooth and sustained gaming experiences.
Does the Realme 15 Pro support expandable storage?
No, it does not have microSD support. Users needing more storage should opt for the 256 GB variant.
Is the Realme 15 Pro available with EMI options?
Yes, EMI options are available starting at ₹1,249/month through finance partners like Bajaj Finserv, HDFC Bank, TVS Credit, and Realme FlexPay.
Can I buy the Realme 15 Pro without a credit card?
Yes, EMI options are available even without a credit card via NBFCs and partners like Realme FlexPay.
