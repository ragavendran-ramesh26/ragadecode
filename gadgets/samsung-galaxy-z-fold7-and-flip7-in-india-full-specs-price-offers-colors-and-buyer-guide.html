Samsung Galaxy Z Fold7 & Flip7 in India: Full Specs, Price, Offers, Colors & Buyer Guide

Published on: 10 Jul 2025

Samsung's newest foldable phones—the Galaxy Z Fold7 and Galaxy Z Flip7—have officially launched in India, with pre-orders now live and general availability starting July 25, 2025. Featuring Galaxy AI + Google Gemini integration, ultra-slim designs, and top-tier specs, these foldables redefine what a smartphone can do.

Whether you're a productivity powerhouse, a trend-savvy creator, or just someone who values innovation, here’s a complete breakdown of everything you need to know.

Galaxy Z Fold7 Specifications

  • Displays:
    • Inner: 8″ QXGA+ AMOLED, 120 Hz
    • Outer: 6.5″ FHD+ AMOLED, 120 Hz
  • Processor: Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 Elite
  • Memory: Up to 16 GB RAM + 1 TB storage
  • Cameras:
    • Rear: 200 MP wide + 12 MP ultra-wide + 10 MP telephoto
    • Front: 10 MP (cover), 16 MP (under-display)
  • Battery: 4,400 mAh, 25 W wired, wireless & reverse wireless charging
  • OS: Android 16 with One UI 8, Galaxy AI + Google Gemini

Galaxy Z Flip7 Specifications

  • Displays:
    • Inner: 6.9″ AMOLED, 120 Hz
    • Outer: 4.1″ Flex Window display
  • Processor: Exynos 2500 (India) / Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 (global)
  • Memory: 12 GB RAM, up to 512 GB storage
  • Cameras:
    • Rear: 50 MP wide + 12 MP ultra-wide
    • Front: 10 MP selfie
  • Battery: 4,300 mAh (up to 31 hours video playback)
  • OS: Android 16 with Google Gemini integration

Top Questions Answered

  1. Should I buy Fold7 or Flip7?

    • Fold7: Ideal for multitasking, productivity, and large-screen use.
    • Flip7: Great for portability, stylish design, and flexible AI camera tools.

  2. Can I use two SIM cards?

    • Yes—both models support 1 Nano SIM + 1 eSIM; Fold7 may support dual physical SIMs in some regions.

  3. Does it support memory cards?

    • No. Opt for higher internal storage when buying.

  4. Can I buy the Mint color offline?

    • No. Mint is exclusive to Samsung’s online store.

  5. Does EMI require a credit card?

    • Not necessarily. Samsung Finance+ and NBFCs offer credit-card-free EMI.

Who Should Buy This?

  • Business Professionals: Galaxy Z Fold7 for split-screen productivity and advanced multitasking.
  • Stylish Content Creators: Galaxy Z Flip7 for its compact size, AI photography features, and modern aesthetics.
  • Budget-Conscious Buyers: Galaxy Z Flip7 FE gives you a foldable experience at a lower cost.
  • Tech Enthusiasts: Both models offer the latest AI, Android 16, and innovative hardware.

Final Verdict

The Galaxy Z Fold7 caters to professionals needing a tablet-sized screen, top-tier performance, and powerful AI tools. The Galaxy Z Flip7 offers a sleek, compact form factor with advanced camera capabilities, AI features, and bold design. With compelling pricing, exclusive pre-order benefits, 0% EMI options, and stylish color selections, Samsung’s 2025 foldables are a standout in innovation and elegance.

Pre-order now at Samsung India or visit your nearest Samsung-authorized retailer.
