vivo X200 FE: Compact Flagship with Big Features

The vivo X200 FE is vivo’s premium compact flagship, aiming to deliver full-featured flagship performance in a pocketable form. It comes with Zeiss-calibrated cameras, a massive 6,500 mAh battery, and advanced AI imaging and software tools—all built into a sleek, lightweight body.

vivo X200 FE Specifications

  • Display

    • 6.31″ LTPO AMOLED, 1.5 K (2640×1216), 120 Hz
    • Peak brightness ~5,000 nits, HDR10+, 2160 Hz PWM dimming

  • Processor

    • MediaTek Dimensity 9300+ (4 nm, all-large cores)

  • Memory & Storage

    • 12 GB / 16 GB LPDDR5X RAM
    • 256 GB / 512 GB UFS 3.1 storage

  • Cameras

    • Rear: 50 MP Sony IMX921 main (OIS) + 50 MP periscope (3× zoom) + 8 MP ultra‑wide
    • Front: 50 MP selfie with autofocus
    • Video: 4K/60 fps with OIS

  • Battery & Charging

    • 6,500 mAh silicon‑anode battery
    • 90 W FlashCharge (0–100% in ~57 min)

  • Build & Design

    • 150.8 × 71.8 × 8 mm, 186 g
    • IP68/IP69 rated
    • Flat metal frame, curved glass back

  • Connectivity & Others

    • 5G, Wi‑Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, NFC
    • Stereo speakers, USB‑C 2.0
    • Under‑display fingerprint scanner

  • Software

    • Android 15 with Funtouch OS 15
    • 4 years of Android updates, 1 year security patch commitment

The vivo X200 FE is the most well-rounded compact flagship of 2025. It combines blazing-fast charging, a stunning display, Zeiss-powered cameras, and AI tools into a refined metal-glass build. If you want flagship power and portability without compromise—this is it.

Quick Info

What is the warranty coverage for the vivo X200 FE?
The device and battery are covered for 12 months, accessories for 6 months, and software updates for 4 years with 1 year of security updates.
What is the vivo X200 FE?
The vivo X200 FE is vivo’s compact flagship smartphone for 2025, designed to offer full-featured performance in a smaller, lightweight body. It features top-tier specifications, AI-enhanced features, and Zeiss-calibrated cameras.
When was the vivo X200 FE launched in India?
The vivo X200 FE officially launched in India on July 14, 2025, alongside the vivo X Fold 5. Sales began on July 23, 2025.
What are the price options for the vivo X200 FE in India?
The vivo X200 FE is priced at ₹54,999 for the 12 GB + 256 GB variant and ₹59,999 for the 16 GB + 512 GB variant. Both versions include a charger and a soft case in the box.
What processor does the vivo X200 FE use?
It uses the MediaTek Dimensity 9300+, a 4nm chipset with all-large core architecture designed to deliver powerful performance and energy efficiency.
How big is the battery on the vivo X200 FE?
The phone is equipped with a large 6,500 mAh silicon-anode battery, supporting fast 90 W FlashCharge that can charge from 0 to 100% in approximately 57 minutes.
What is the display quality of the vivo X200 FE?
The phone features a 6.31-inch LTPO AMOLED display with a 1.5 K resolution, 120 Hz refresh rate, HDR10+ support, 2160 Hz PWM dimming, and a peak brightness of around 5,000 nits.
What camera system does the vivo X200 FE have?
It offers a triple rear camera setup: 50 MP Sony IMX921 main sensor with OIS, a 50 MP periscope camera with 3× zoom, and an 8 MP ultra-wide camera. The front camera is a 50 MP sensor with autofocus.
Does the vivo X200 FE support 4K video recording?
Yes, the vivo X200 FE supports 4K video recording at 60 fps with optical image stabilization for smoother footage.
What are the AI features in the vivo X200 FE?
It includes AI-powered imaging tools like object removal and filters, as well as smart features like on-device call transcription, document scanning, translation, and Google Gemini AI tools like Circle to Search and Smart Reply.
What is the software experience on the vivo X200 FE?
The phone runs on Android 15 with vivo’s Funtouch OS 15, offering a refined user interface with four years of Android updates and one year of security patch support.
Is the vivo X200 FE waterproof?
Yes, the phone has an IP68/IP69 rating, meaning it is dust-tight and protected against water immersion and high-pressure water jets.
What materials are used in the vivo X200 FE design?
It has a flat metal frame and a curved glass back, offering a premium and ergonomic design in a compact form factor.
What are the colour options available for the vivo X200 FE?
The phone is available in three colour variants: Luxe Grey, Amber Yellow, and a dual-tone Pastel Blue & Pink.
What accessories are included in the vivo X200 FE box?
The box includes the vivo X200 FE device, a 90 W charger, a USB-C cable, a transparent TPU case, a SIM ejector tool, and a Quick Start Guide.

In-Depth Answers

How heavy and compact is the vivo X200 FE?
The phone weighs 186 g and has dimensions of 150.8 × 71.8 × 8 mm, making it one of the most compact and lightweight flagship smartphones of 2025.
What memory and storage variants are offered for the vivo X200 FE?
It comes in two configurations: 12 GB RAM with 256 GB storage and 16 GB RAM with 512 GB storage, both using LPDDR5X RAM and UFS 3.1 storage.
What kind of users is the vivo X200 FE best suited for?
It is ideal for users seeking a compact flagship, photography enthusiasts, frequent travelers needing long battery life, and those who value smart AI features and software longevity.
What makes the vivo X200 FE stand out in 2025?
It combines high-end performance, a stellar camera system, advanced AI capabilities, and a large battery in a compact design, making it the most well-rounded compact flagship of the year.
What kind of fingerprint scanner does the vivo X200 FE have?
The phone features an under-display fingerprint scanner for secure and fast unlocking.
Does the vivo X200 FE support 5G connectivity?
Yes, the phone includes 5G connectivity along with Wi‑Fi 7, Bluetooth 5.4, and NFC.
What safety features does the vivo X200 FE battery include?
It features smart charging control and is SGS certified for low-blue light and safe battery operation.
What software tools enhance productivity on the vivo X200 FE?
The device includes on-device call transcription, document scanning, and translation tools, boosting productivity without relying on external apps.
How long does the vivo X200 FE take to fully charge?
With its 90 W FlashCharge technology, the vivo X200 FE can charge from 0 to 100% in approximately 57 minutes.
What version of Android does the vivo X200 FE ship with?
It comes with Android 15 out of the box, paired with vivo’s custom Funtouch OS 15 interface.
Does the vivo X200 FE include stereo speakers?
Yes, the phone is equipped with stereo speakers for enhanced audio playback during media consumption and calls.
What is the resolution of the vivo X200 FE Telephoto camera?
The vivo X200 FE features a 50 MP telephoto camera with a periscope lens setup. It supports 3× optical zoom, allowing users to capture detailed zoomed-in images without compromising image quality.
Is the vivo X200 FE an AI phone?
Yes, the vivo X200 FE qualifies as an AI phone due to its integration of advanced AI features. These include AI-powered imaging tools like object removal and filters, smart on-device utilities for call transcription, document scanning, and translation, as well as Google Gemini AI features like Circle to Search and Smart Reply. Its AI-based battery management also contributes to safety and longevity, enhancing the overall user experience.
