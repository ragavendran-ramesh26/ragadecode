Samsung Galaxy F36 5G – Budget-Friendly AI Phone Launching in India on July 19, 2025

Published on: 16 Jul 2025

The Galaxy F36 5G is Samsung’s next AI phone in India, bringing Galaxy AI features to the budget-friendly segment. With its “Flex Hi‑FAI” tagline, Flipkart teaser, and a vegan-leather finish, the phone blends stylish design, smart imaging tools, and strong performance. Powered by the Exynos 1380 SoC, One UI 7 on Android 15, and a 6.7″ FHD+ 120 Hz AMOLED display, it delivers a flagship-like experience at an affordable price.

Top Questions Answered

1. What makes the F36 an AI phone?
It features Samsung’s Flex Hi‑FAI tools like Edit Suggestions, Object Eraser, and AI-powered Night Mode—all processed on-device.

2. Is the design premium?
Yes. A vegan leather finish with slim 7.7 mm thickness adds a premium feel at an affordable price.

3. Does the battery last all day?
Yes. The 5,000 mAh battery paired with One UI optimizations should comfortably last a full day on moderate use.

4. Will it get long-term software support?
Yes. Samsung is offering 7 Android updates and 6 years of security patches—best in class for a budget phone.

5. Where can I buy it?
It’s launching on Flipkart with expected launch day deals and EMI plans.

Who Should Buy This?

  • Budget-conscious buyers looking for AI features under ₹20K
  • Students & young professionals needing a reliable daily driver
  • Content sharers who want AI tools like background eraser and night shot
  • Samsung loyalists looking for long-term Android support

Final Verdict

The Samsung Galaxy F36 5G stands out as one of the most capable AI phones in the sub-₹20,000 segment. With its flagship-like display, smart photography tools, and long-term update promise, it's an excellent choice for budget buyers who don’t want to compromise on modern tech.
