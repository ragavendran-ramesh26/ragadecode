The Galaxy F36 5G is Samsung’s next AI phone in India, bringing Galaxy AI features to the budget-friendly segment. With its “Flex Hi‑FAI” tagline, Flipkart teaser, and a vegan-leather finish, the phone blends stylish design, smart imaging tools, and strong performance.
