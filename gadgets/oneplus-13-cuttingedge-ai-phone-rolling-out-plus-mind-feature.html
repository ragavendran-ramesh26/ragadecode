The OnePlus 13 is positioning itself as a powerful AI phone, combining flagship hardware with intelligent software features. Its standout innovation is Plus Mind—a voice- and context-aware AI assistant that turns the phone into a proactive organizer. Whether you're a productivity-focused professional or someone who values seamless AI integration, the OnePlus 13 delivers future‑ready intelligence backed by Snapdragon 8 Elite performance.

From voice summaries and image captures to smart scheduling and translation, the OnePlus 13 isn’t just about power—it’s about how smartly that power is used.

Top Questions Answered

1. Is Plus Mind a free feature?
Yes, Plus Mind is included in the latest software update for OnePlus 13, 13R, and 13s—no subscription required.

2. How do I activate Plus Mind?
Swipe up with 3 fingers on any screen, or press the physical Plus Key on OnePlus 13s models.

3. Is OnePlus 13 an AI phone?
Yes. It runs multiple on-device AI features like summarization, scheduling, editing, and translation—all powered by Snapdragon’s advanced AI engine.

4. Does it support wireless charging?
Yes—50W fast wireless charging and 100W wired charging.

5. Can I use Google Gemini with OnePlus 13?
Yes. Gemini integration is rolling out and is directly accessible on OnePlus 13s lockscreen. On OnePlus 13, you can install Gemini from the Play Store.

Who Should Buy This?

  • Professionals & Power Users – Plus Mind makes real-time research and scheduling effortless.
  • Tech Enthusiasts – Snapdragon 8 Gen 3, LTPO QHD display, fast storage.
  • Content Creators – Triple 50 MP camera, Hasselblad color tuning, and 4K video.
  • AI First-Movers – Fully integrated AI workflow system on a top-tier Android device.

Final Verdict

The OnePlus 13 is no longer just a performance beast—it’s a next-gen AI phone built for the future. With the new Plus Mind update, it becomes a proactive assistant, content organizer, and smart device all-in-one. If you're looking for a flagship that intelligently adapts to your needs—this is the one to beat in 2025.

Quick Info

Is OnePlus 13 future-proof in terms of software updates?
Yes, it receives 4 years of Android updates and 6 years of security patches, making it future-ready.
What type of charging port does the OnePlus 13 use?
It uses a USB-C 3.1 port, which also supports reverse wired charging.
What is the OnePlus 13?
The OnePlus 13 is a flagship Android smartphone equipped with powerful hardware and advanced AI features like the Plus Mind assistant. It's designed for users who want both high performance and intelligent software capabilities.
When was the OnePlus 13 launched?
The OnePlus 13 had its global launch on January 7, 2025, and the Plus Mind AI feature was rolled out to the 13 series on July 15, 2025.
What is the Plus Mind feature in OnePlus 13?
Plus Mind is a context-aware AI assistant that captures content from your screen and organizes it into a 'Mind Space'. It can auto-generate calendar events, translate text, and summarize information using voice or touch gestures.
How do you activate Plus Mind on the OnePlus 13?
You can activate Plus Mind by swiping up with three fingers on any screen. OnePlus 13s users can also use the physical Plus Key.
Is Plus Mind available for free?
Yes, Plus Mind is a free feature included in the latest software update for OnePlus 13, 13R, and 13s devices.
Does the OnePlus 13 support wireless charging?
Yes, the OnePlus 13 supports 50W fast wireless charging along with 100W SUPERVOOC wired charging.
What processor powers the OnePlus 13?
It is powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, branded as Snapdragon 8 Elite, offering top-tier performance and AI capabilities.
What are the camera specs of the OnePlus 13?
The phone features a triple 50 MP rear camera setup (main, ultra-wide, periscope telephoto) and a 32 MP front camera capable of 4K video.
What is the battery capacity of the OnePlus 13?
The OnePlus 13 has a 6,000 mAh silicon-carbon battery that can charge from 0 to 100% in about 36 minutes with 100W wired charging.
What display does the OnePlus 13 use?
It features a 6.82″ LTPO AMOLED display with QHD+ resolution, 120 Hz refresh rate, HDR10+, Dolby Vision, and peak brightness of 4500 nits.
What variants of OnePlus 13 are available in India?
Variants include 12 GB + 256 GB, 16 GB + 512 GB, and 24 GB + 1 TB, priced at ₹54,999+, ₹59,999+, and ₹69,999+ respectively.
Does the OnePlus 13 support expandable storage?
No, the OnePlus 13 does not support microSD cards. Users should choose higher internal storage variants if needed.
What AI features are included in the OnePlus AI Suite?
Features include AI VoiceScribe for transcription, AI Call Assistant, one-tap translation, AI Reframe for photos, and Gemini AI integration.
